Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

Nothing you type into the email builder is ever transmitted to a server or stored anywhere outside your browser. All content — section text, titles, details, and settings — exists only in your browser's memory for the duration of your session and is discarded when you close or refresh the page.

Analytics

We may use third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how the app is used — for example, how many people visit or which features are used most. These tools may collect anonymous data such as your browser type, approximate location, and pages visited. They do not have access to any content you enter into the builder.

Cookies

This app does not set any cookies of its own. Analytics tools, if active, may set their own cookies in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

Third-party services

This tool is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical information such as IP addresses as part of normal web hosting. See GitHub's privacy statement for details.

Contact

This tool is not affiliated with Trail Life USA and is provided as a free resource to Trail Life troops. Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to the tool's maintainer via the project's GitHub page.
